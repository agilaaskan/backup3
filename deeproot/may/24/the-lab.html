From Bud to Concentrates

Deep Roots Harvest Extracts uses 100% Nevada grown cannabis and are lab-tested to ensure the cleanest products. We offer unique vape blends of C02 distillate and BHO wax concentrates. We use cutting edge technology run by our knowledgeable and experienced team.

Deep Roots Harvest Labs launched in 2018 with our vape oils. We use premium quality bud to produce the finest distillate oil using the CO2 extraction method. CO2 extraction uses pressurized carbon dioxide to take as many desirable cannabinoids from a marijuana. Once our C02 oil is ready, we reintroduce our terpenes to the distillate. This is how we get our custom unique blends. We use zero curing agents such as PEG’s, glycerin, MCT and other chemicals. You can find these available in 500mg vape cartridges or disposable vape pens.

With our state-of-the-art extraction booth we are able to produce wax concentrate using the BHO extraction method. BHO is short for butane hash oil, a cannabis oil that comes from using butane as a solvent. We produce multiple consistencies using this method such as shatter, sugar and batter. Shatter has a hard, smooth, glass-like consistency that can easily be broken into shards. Sugar has a wet, sappy consistency. Batter is similar to budder and badder with golden creamy texture. We are constantly producing new concentrates such as diamonds and crumble.

Our wax comes out beautifully golden with the perfect constancy. You will be hit with intoxicating aromas right when you open the jar not to mention the incredible flavor once heated. You can find our live resin packaged in glass jars and our cured resin packaged in a slim container in our heat-sealed pouches.

Our Lab is constantly working hard to provide you with the best of the best. Join us in our mission to provide you with the cleanest and most effective concentrates possible.



Everything we do is about the best. From the farm, the kitchen and the lab, to our crew members who know exactly what you need – everything we do is with the goal to be the very best.
