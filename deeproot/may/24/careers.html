CAREERS AT DEEP ROOTS HARVEST
Why Deep Roots Harvest? Growth, purpose, and people. For starters.
If you think the Cannabis industry is all about cultivation, great products, innovation, helping patients and delighting customers, you’re right. But at Deep Roots Harvest, there’s a wide range of opportunities available to you -opportunities to play a key role in the success of our organization.

Be on the front lines and interact with patients and customers as a Budtender or Brand Ambassador. Maybe you prefer to work behind the scenes to keep things ticking in Accounts Payable or as a Maintenance Engineer. At Deep Roots Harvest there is a career path for any interest & level of experience.

HOW DO I APPLY FOR A JOB AT YOUR WENDOVER LOCATION?
All positions that we are seeking outside applicants for will be posted here. When you click on the “View Job Openings” button above, you can view job descriptions and details for open jobs. If there are no jobs listed or “Wendover, NV” is not listed as an option under “Location”, that means we do not have any open positions available and are not yet hiring for our Wendover location.

We do not accept or keep resume’s on file for positions that are not listed as open or available.
