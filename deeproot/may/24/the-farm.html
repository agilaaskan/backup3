Farm To Body

Deep Roots Harvest cannabis has been proudly cultivated and packaged in Mesquite, Nevada, since 2015. We are one of the largest growers in the state, having supplied over 80% of the dispensaries in Nevada. Our team of experienced growers aim to enhance our environment and maximize the full potential of every harvest. We consistently produce high-quality cannabis flowers for our consumers.

Deep Roots Harvest flower is cultivated with a state-of-the-art, indoor grow system. That system along with our valued grow team help to maintain environmental and nutritional conditions to produce the maximum amount of quality marijuana from each plant. Our highly experienced horticulturists do not use any harmful chemical enhancements or unnecessary shortcuts. We hand trim our flower to ensure individual care is given to each product, then packed in our smell proof heat sealed pouches ensure fresh quality flower every time.

Our flower goes through various stages of its life cycle. Once we get our clones planted, they go into the vegetation stage. During this stage the stem of the plant gets thicker and taller. Then it will hit the pre flowering stage and from there to the flowering stage. The flowering stage can take anywhere from eight to ten weeks. The last and most essential stage is the harvesting stage! This is the stage where we cut our plant branches. Once we get our branches cut, we put them in a dark cool room and hang them upside down. After about five to six days we begin the trimming and packaging process. Our packaging team then packages the loose flower as well as our bluebird pre-roll joints.



Everything we do is about the best. From the farm, the kitchen and the lab, to our crew members who know exactly what you need – everything we do is with the goal to be the very best.
